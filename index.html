Guess Game

Let's play a game!

To start playing, enter in the first field your minimum value from 1 to 199. In the second field, enter a value from 2 to 200. In the third field, enter the number of attempts from 1 to 15 and click Start.

Enter in the input field your value from minimum to maximum value and press the Generate button. If you want to end the game, click the exit button.
